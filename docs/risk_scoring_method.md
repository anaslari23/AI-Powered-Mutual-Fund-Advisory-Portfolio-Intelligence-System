# Risk Scoring Method

Risk scores are generated algorithmically on a scale of 0 to 10.

## Component Weights
- **Age Factor**: 30%
- **Dependents Factor**: 20%
- **Income Stability**: 20%
- **Behavioral Stability**: 30%

## Classification Boundaries
- `< 5`: Conservative
- `5 - 7`: Moderate
- `> 7`: Aggressive
